Fawkes the Phoenix

> Phoenixes burst into flame when it is time for them to die and are reborn from the ashes. >

  • Breed: Phoenix

  • Appearance: Red and Gold Feathers

  • Magic Abilities: Ability to burst into flame when their bodies begin to fail, then be reborn from the ashes. Can carry incredibly heavy loads, tears have healing properties

  • Allegiance: Domesticated phoenixes are loyal to their owners
